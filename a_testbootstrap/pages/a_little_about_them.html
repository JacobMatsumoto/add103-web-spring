A little about them

Butts and Taco are best friends! ...when they chose to be... Butts is a very hyper active little guy, he has feline pica and will eat anything! Taco is very reserved but once he gets to like you he loves pets.

Frank is my grandma's cat. He's named after Frank Sinatra. When she first got him he was an absolute scaredy cat but has since come out of his shell a ton and is always following her

Martini! Martini is a baby cat we fostered for a few months, we found her outside at about 1-2 weeks old and helped nurture her back to health, she is a fighter! She fought so many illnesses and we're so glad we helped her get her forever home!
