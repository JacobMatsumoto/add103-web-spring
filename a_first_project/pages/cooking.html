Cooking

I have always loved to cook, I’d even go so far as to say I’m pretty good at it. I don’t cook anything fancy, but I can whip something up on the fly, and nine times out of ten, it’s pretty damn good. Back in high school, I took a couple of culinary classes and even earned my manager’s certification for working in a kitchen. Since then, I’ve spent about four or five years in the industry. While I don’t see a career in it anymore, I’m still grateful to do it for a living. It really is a passion of mine. One of my favorite things to cook is pasta.
